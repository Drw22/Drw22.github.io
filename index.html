Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution

There are a lot of generations of lancer evolution out there like

  • 1992 - 1994 Lancer Evolution I
  • 1994 - 1995 Lancer Evolution II
  • 1995 - 1996 Lancer Evolution III
  • 1997 - 1998 Lancer Evolution IV
  • 1998 Lancer Evolution V
  • 1999 Lancer Evolution VI
  • 2000 - 2001 Lancer Evolution VI TME

The first Lancer Evolution used the 2.0 L turbocharged DOHC engine and AWD drivetrain from the original Galant VR-4 in a Lancer chassis, and was sold in GSR and RS models.
